Biography

Known for her artistry, elegance, and technical prowess, Kostornaia switched from women's singles to pairs. She was the 2020 European champion, 2020 Grand Prix Final champion, and a six-time Grand Prix medalist. At the junior level, she won silver at the 2018 Junior World Championships and gold at the 2018–19 Junior Grand Prix Final. Kostornaia is one of the few women in history to successfully land a triple Axel in senior competition.
